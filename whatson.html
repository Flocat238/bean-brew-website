What's On

START FROM SCRATCH

Saturday 28th February 2026

Known for their energetic performances, Start From Scratch are a fantastic pop-punk/emo band who have recently supported other local bands like Rare Breed and Mouse. They'll be filling our event space with tracks from their fantastic new album, due to be released soon. Come along and experience them for yourselves - just £5 entry and all profits go to the bands chosen charity.

Mondays

Dungeons & Dragons

Start the week with a little adventure! whether you're a seasoned player or brand new, our D&D nights are all about storytelling, imagination, and having fun together.

Tuesdays

Book Club

A cosy evening for book lovers. Join us to chat about our current read, share thoughts, and enjoy good conversation over great coffee - no pressure, just pages and people.

Wednesdays

Games Night

From board games to card games and everything in between, Games Night is all about friendly competition, laughter, and good company.

Thursdays

Knitting for Knitwits

No experience required - promise. A welcoming night for knitters of all levels (including total beginners) to learn, laugh, and get tangled in yarn together.

Fridays

Paint & Sip

Unwind at the end of the week with a brush in one hand and a drink in the other. A fun, relaxed evening of creativity where perfection definitely isn't required.

Saturdays

Open Mic Night

Our space comes alive with music, poetry, comedy, and all kinds of talent. Whether you're performing or just soaking it all in, everyone is welcome.

Sundays

Craft & Crochet

Slow Sundays are made for crafting. Bring your crochet, embroidery, or any creative project and enjoy a calm, cosy end to the week with like-minded makers.
